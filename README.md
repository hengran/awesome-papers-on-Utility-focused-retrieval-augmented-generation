# Concept of Relevance and Utility in IR
  1. A definition of relevance for information retrieval， W.S. Cooper， 1971    
     The **first paper proposed the utility concept in IR**: "Relevance is largely an unexplicated concept. It seems natural to make at the start a rough distinction between what has been called logicarelevance, alias “topic-appropriateness”, which has to do with whether or not a piece of information is on a subject which has some topical bearing on the information need in question, and utility, which has to do with the ultimate usefulness of the piece of information to the user. "
